OUR MISSION

Westside is all about providing a home for volleyball players to play, learn and have fun. Whether you are still discovering the game, or a veteran of the sport, we'll make sure that you feel like a part of the Westside crew. We know that in NSW it can be hard to find a place to play and improve in the west, so we try and cater to all of the players out in the Sydney South West region. Check out where you can find us in the locations section.



OUR TEAMS

WOMEN

We have two womens teams at Westside, our Honours level girls and the Division 2 girls. As with all of our teams, the women play at tournaments, travelling around Austrlia as well as in the Sydney Volleyball League (SVL).

JUNIOURS

At Westside we have a lot of juniour and development teams which train to improve and then play in competetions around town. Some of the juniours play in SVL as a part of our mens and womens teams.

MEN

We have a lot of Mens teams of ranging divisions which compete in tounraments as well as SVL.
